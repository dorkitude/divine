"Everything we hear is an opinion, not a fact. Everything we see is a perspective, not the truth. How much time he gains who does not look to see what his neighbor says or does."

— Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book VI

Stop watching the audience. Focus on the work. Reputation is smoke; character is fire.
