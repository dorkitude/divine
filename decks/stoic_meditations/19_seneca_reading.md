"Be careful, however, lest this reading of many authors and books of every sort tend to make you discursive. Linger among a limited number of master thinkers and digest their works."

— Seneca, *Letters to Lucilius*, 2

Shallow reading of many books teaches less than deep reading of a few. Let wisdom penetrate rather than merely pass through.
