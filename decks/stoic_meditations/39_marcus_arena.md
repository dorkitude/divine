"The art of living is more like wrestling than dancing — in that it stands ready against whatever comes and is not thrown by the unforeseen."

— Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book VII

Life is not a choreographed performance. It is a match against an unpredictable opponent. Stay grounded and respond.
