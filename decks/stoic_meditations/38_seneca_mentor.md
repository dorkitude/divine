"Choose someone whose way of life, conversation, and face all please you. Picture them always to yourself as your protector or your pattern. We need someone to measure ourselves against."

— Seneca, *Letters to Lucilius*, 11

An internal compass needs calibration. A worthy example provides the reference point.
