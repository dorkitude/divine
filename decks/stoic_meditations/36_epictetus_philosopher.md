"Do not call yourself a philosopher. Do not talk to others about your principles — show them by your actions. Sheep do not vomit up grass to show the shepherds how much they have eaten. They digest it and produce wool and milk."

— Epictetus, *Enchiridion*, 46

Digest your philosophy. Let it come out as changed behavior, not speeches about changed behavior.
