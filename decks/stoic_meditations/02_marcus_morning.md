"When you wake in the morning, tell yourself: the people I deal with today will be meddling, ungrateful, arrogant, dishonest, jealous, and surly. They are like this because they cannot tell good from evil."

— Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book II

Prepare for difficulty each morning and you will not be ambushed by it. Others act from ignorance, not malice.
