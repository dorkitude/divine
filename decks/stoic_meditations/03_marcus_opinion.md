"Choose not to be harmed — and you will not feel harmed. Do not feel harmed — and you have not been."

— Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book IV

Suffering begins with the opinion you attach to events, not the events themselves. Remove the judgment and the pain dissolves.
