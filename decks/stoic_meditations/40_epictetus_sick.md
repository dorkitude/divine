"In illness, do not lose what you have gained in health. Be patient with the doctor, kind to those who tend you, and untroubled by the fever. If you practice these things, what more could health itself have given you?"

— Epictetus, *Discourses*, III.10

Virtue is not something you practice only when comfortable. Its real test comes in difficulty.
