"Nothing is more honorable than a grateful heart. Even among those who are loaded with wealth, the richest person is the one who is content with what fortune has given."

— Seneca, *Letters to Lucilius*

Comparing yourself to others steals the enjoyment of what you already possess. Run your own race.
