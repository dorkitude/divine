"Alexander the Great and his mule driver were both brought to the same place by death. They were either received into the same generative principles of the universe or scattered alike into atoms."

— Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book VI

Fame and obscurity arrive at the same destination. This is not nihilism — it is liberation from the tyranny of status.
