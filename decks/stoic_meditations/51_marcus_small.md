"Look at the stars and consider that you are running your course alongside them. Constantly meditate on the rapid change of things in nature. The universe is transformation; life is opinion."

— Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book VI

Zoom out far enough and your problems become specks. This is not minimization — it is proper scale.
