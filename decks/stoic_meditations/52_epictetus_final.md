"Make it your study to confront every impression with the words: 'You are but an impression and not at all the thing you appear to be.' Then test it by the rules you possess — and first by this: whether it concerns things that are within our power or things that are not."

— Epictetus, *Discourses*, I.1

This is the foundational practice. Before every reaction, pause. Examine. Then choose your response with full awareness.
