"At dawn, when you have trouble getting out of bed, tell yourself: I have to go to work — as a human being. What do I have to complain about if I am going to do what I was born for — the things which I was brought into the world to do?"

— Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book V

You were not born for comfort. You were born for contribution. Get up.
