"Philosophy is not a trick to catch the public. It is not devised for show. It is a matter of the soul — not of the school. Its purpose is to cure."

— Seneca, *Letters to Lucilius*, 16

Philosophy that does not heal the soul is mere decoration. Apply it or discard it.
