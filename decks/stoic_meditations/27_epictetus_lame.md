"Lameness is an impediment to the leg, but not to the will. Say this to yourself with regard to everything that happens and you will find it an impediment to something else, but not truly to yourself."

— Epictetus, *Enchiridion*, 9

External limitations restrict external things. They cannot touch the inner citadel unless you let them in.
