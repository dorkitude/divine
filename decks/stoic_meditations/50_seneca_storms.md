"The pilot cannot prevent the storm, but they can choose not to put out to sea in one. Plan for difficulty in calm, and the storm will find you ready."

— Seneca, *Letters to Lucilius*

Wisdom is preparation made in peaceful times. When the storm hits, it is too late to learn seamanship.
