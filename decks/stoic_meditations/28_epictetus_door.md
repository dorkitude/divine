"Has someone made smoke in the house? If it is moderate, I will stay. If it is too much, I leave. Remember that the door is always open."

— Epictetus, *Discourses*, I.25

You always have a choice. Knowing the door is open makes it easier to stay — because staying becomes voluntary.
