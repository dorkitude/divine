"People look for retreats — in the country, at the seashore, in the mountains. But it is always possible to retreat into yourself. For there is no retreat more peaceful or less troubled than one's own soul."

— Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book IV

The sanctuary you seek is already built. It is within you. Visit it often.
