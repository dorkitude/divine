"Some things are within our power, while others are not. Within our power are opinion, motivation, desire, and aversion. Not within our power are our body, property, reputation, and position."

— Epictetus, *Enchiridion*, 1

This is the foundation of all Stoic practice. Master this distinction and you have mastered the essential skill of a tranquil life.
