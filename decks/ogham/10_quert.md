ᚊ — Quert (Apple)
Letter value: Q

The apple tree is the tree of the Celtic Otherworld — Avalon, the isle of apples. Quert represents beauty, wholeness, and the choices that determine which world you inhabit. Abundance is present, but it requires discernment. Choose well. Not every beautiful fruit nourishes equally.
