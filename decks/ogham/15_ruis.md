ᚏ — Ruis (Elder)
Letter value: R

The elder tree is associated with endings, the Cailleach (the crone goddess), and the completion of cycles. Ruis represents the wisdom that comes from reaching the end of a phase. Something is finishing. Honor what it was, release it with grace, and trust that the cycle will begin again.
