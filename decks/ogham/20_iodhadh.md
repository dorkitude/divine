ᚔ — Iodhadh (Yew)
Letter value: I

The yew lives for thousands of years, its inner wood dying and regenerating continuously. Iodhadh represents the eternal cycle of death and rebirth, connection to ancestors, and the deep time that makes individual lifespans seem brief. What endures is not the body but the pattern. You are part of something ancient and ongoing.
