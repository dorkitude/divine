ᚇ — Duir (Oak)
Letter value: D

The oak is the king of the forest — strong, enduring, and generous in its shelter. The word "door" shares its root with Duir. This is the tree of sovereignty, truth, and solid ground. You have the strength to weather what comes. Stand in your authority. The door is open to those who have earned the right to pass through.
