ᚑ — Onn (Gorse)
Letter value: O

Gorse blooms golden even in the poorest soil and the harshest weather. Onn represents irrepressible vitality, hope that persists against all odds, and the attractive power of genuine enthusiasm. Even in difficult circumstances, something bright and alive is pushing through. Let it.
