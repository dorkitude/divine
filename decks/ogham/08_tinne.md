ᚈ — Tinne (Holly)
Letter value: T

Holly keeps its green through the darkest winter. Tinne represents the warrior's spirit — not aggression, but the disciplined energy needed to maintain balance under pressure. It speaks to challenges met with skill and directed force. Channel your energy precisely. Strike only where it matters.
