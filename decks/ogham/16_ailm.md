ᚐ — Ailm (Pine/Elm)
Letter value: A

The pine stands tall above the forest canopy, offering a vantage point over the landscape below. Ailm represents clarity of vision, elevated perspective, and the endurance to see far into the distance. Rise above the immediate confusion. From a higher vantage, the path becomes clear.
