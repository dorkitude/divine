ᚓ — Eadhadh (Aspen)
Letter value: E

The aspen trembles at the slightest breeze — its leaves are in constant motion. Eadhadh represents sensitivity to what others miss, the courage to remain open even when afraid, and communication that happens below the threshold of words. Fear is not weakness. It is awareness sharpened to a fine edge.
