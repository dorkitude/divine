ᚃ — Fearn (Alder)
Letter value: F

The alder thrives at the water's edge, bridging land and water. Fearn represents the ability to navigate between worlds — practical and spiritual, known and unknown. It offers guidance and a stable foundation from which to explore uncertain territory. Stand firm where others cannot.
