ᚉ — Coll (Hazel)
Letter value: C

In Celtic legend, the salmon of wisdom ate hazelnuts that fell into the sacred pool. Coll is the tree of knowledge, poetic inspiration, and creative insight. It encourages study, curiosity, and the pursuit of genuine understanding. The answers you seek are available — look beneath the surface.
