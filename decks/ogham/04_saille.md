ᚄ — Saille (Willow)
Letter value: S

The willow bends with the water and the wind, never breaking. Saille is the tree of intuition, emotion, and lunar cycles. It counsels flexibility and responsiveness to the currents of feeling moving through your life. Yield where you must. Rigidity is not strength — adaptability is.
