ᚒ — Úr (Heather)
Letter value: U

Heather blankets the highlands in purple, transforming barren moors into landscapes of unexpected beauty. Úr represents passion, healing, and the generous spirit that turns harsh ground into something lovely. Give freely of yourself. Your warmth changes the landscape for everyone around you.
