ᚋ — Muin (Vine)
Letter value: M

The vine produces the grape, and the grape produces wine — the drink of prophecy and uninhibited truth. Muin represents harvest, celebration, and the release of inner truth that comes when inhibitions fall away. Speak honestly. Let the intoxication of authenticity carry you beyond your usual caution.
