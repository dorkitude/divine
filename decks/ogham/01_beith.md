ᚁ — Beith (Birch)
Letter value: B

The birch is the pioneer tree, first to reclaim barren ground after fire or clearing. Beith signals fresh starts, purification, and the courage to begin again. Sweep away what is old and stale. The ground is ready for something new, and you are the one to plant it.
