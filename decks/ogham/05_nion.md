ᚅ — Nion (Ash)
Letter value: N

The ash is the world tree of Celtic and Norse cosmology — its roots reach into the deep, its branches touch the sky. Nion represents the interconnection of all things, the web of fate that links cause to consequence. See the larger pattern. Your actions ripple outward further than you know.
