ᚂ — Luis (Rowan)
Letter value: L

The rowan tree was planted at doorways to ward off harmful spirits. Luis represents protection through clear vision and sharp discernment. It asks you to distinguish between what is real and what is illusion. Trust your ability to see through deception — your instincts are sound.
