ᚆ — Huath (Hawthorn)
Letter value: H

The hawthorn blooms beautifully but is armed with thorns. Huath represents a period of testing and necessary restraint. Not every door should be opened immediately. Some things need time to ripen, and rushing forward could draw blood. Practice patience. Wait for the right moment.
