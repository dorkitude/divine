ᚌ — Gort (Ivy)
Letter value: G

Ivy spirals and climbs, finding purchase on the most inhospitable surfaces. Gort represents tenacious growth, the relentless search for light, and the spiral path of inner development. Progress may not be linear, but it is real. Keep climbing. The indirect route is sometimes the only route.
