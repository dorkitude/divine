ᚍ — nGétal (Reed/Broom)
Letter value: NG

Reeds were used to make arrows — instruments of direct, purposeful action. nGétal represents taking aim, finding your target, and acting with precision and unity of purpose. Scattered effort wastes energy. Identify what matters most and direct yourself toward it like an arrow in flight.
