ᚎ — Straif (Blackthorn)
Letter value: ST/Z

The blackthorn is fierce — thorned, dark-barked, blooming before it even has leaves. Straif represents necessary hardship, the authority that comes from having endured difficulty, and the transformative power of fate. This is not a gentle card. Something difficult must be faced, and facing it will forge you.
