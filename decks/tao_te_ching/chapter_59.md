In governing people and serving heaven, nothing exceeds moderation. Moderation means returning early to the Tao. Returning early means accumulating virtue. With accumulated virtue, nothing is impossible. When nothing is impossible, no limit is known. One whose limits are unknown can govern a country. One who governs with the mother principle endures long.
