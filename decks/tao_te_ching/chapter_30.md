---
title: "Force Is Followed by Loss of Strength"
number: 30
keywords: ["force", "war", "restraint", "consequence"]
asset_url: ""
---

Whoever relies on the Tao in governing does not use force against the world. For every force there is a counterforce. Where armies have camped, thorns and brambles grow. After every great war, years of famine follow. Achieve results, then stop. Do not use force to dominate. Achieve results without arrogance, without boasting, without pride — achieve results because they were necessary, not because you wanted to display your power.
