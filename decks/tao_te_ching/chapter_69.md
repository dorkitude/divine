The strategists have a saying: "I dare not be the host but prefer to be the guest. I dare not advance an inch but prefer to retreat a foot." This is called marching without marching, rolling up sleeves without baring arms, grasping without weapons, advancing without an enemy. There is no greater disaster than underestimating the opponent. When two evenly matched forces meet, the one that grieves will win.
