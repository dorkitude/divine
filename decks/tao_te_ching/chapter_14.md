Look for it and you cannot see it — it is called the invisible. Listen for it and you cannot hear it — it is called the inaudible. Reach for it and you cannot grasp it — it is called the subtle. These three cannot be further examined, so they merge into one. It is not bright above or dark below. It is the form of the formless, the image of nothing. Hold to the ancient Tao and you can manage present existence.
