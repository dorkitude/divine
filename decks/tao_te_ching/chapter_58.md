When government is dull and sleepy, the people are wholesome. When government is sharp and alert, the people are cunning. Fortune rests upon misfortune. Misfortune hides beneath fortune. Who knows where the turning point lies? What is normal becomes strange. What is good becomes harmful. People have been confused about this for a long time.
