---
title: "Return to the Root"
number: 16
keywords: ["stillness", "return", "root", "constancy"]
asset_url: ""
---

Attain the utmost emptiness. Hold firm to stillness. The ten thousand things rise and fall while the self watches their return. They grow and flourish, then each returns to the root. Returning to the root is stillness, which is the way of nature. Knowing constancy is insight. Not knowing constancy leads to disaster.
