Heaven and earth are not sentimental — they treat all things as straw dogs, equal and expendable. The sage follows this pattern, treating people without favoritism. The space between heaven and earth is like a bellows: empty yet inexhaustible, always producing more. Too many words lead to exhaustion. Better to stay centered.
