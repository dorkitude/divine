---
title: "Weapons Are Tools of Fear"
number: 31
keywords: ["weapons", "peace", "sorrow", "restraint"]
asset_url: ""
---

Good weapons are instruments of fear — all creatures hate them. Therefore followers of the Tao avoid them. The sage prefers the left side (the side of peace); in war, the right side (the side of force). Weapons are not the sage's tools. When compelled to use them, restraint is best. There is no glory in victory. Those who glorify victory delight in the slaughter of people.
