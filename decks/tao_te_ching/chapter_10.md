Can you embrace your soul and hold to unity without separation? Can you concentrate your vital force and become soft as a newborn? Can you clean your inner vision until you see nothing but the light? Can you love people and govern without cleverness? Opening and closing the gates of heaven, can you play the part of the feminine? Understanding all, can you step back from your own knowledge?
