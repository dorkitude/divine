Those that attained the One in ancient times: heaven attained the One and became clear; earth attained the One and became stable; spirits attained the One and became powerful. Without clarity, heaven would crack. Without stability, earth would shake. The noble person has their foundation in the humble. The high has its base in the low. This is why rulers call themselves orphaned and desolate.
