---
title: "The Way of Heaven Is Like Bending a Bow"
number: 77
keywords: ["balance", "heaven", "excess", "redistribution"]
asset_url: ""
---

The way of heaven is like bending a bow. The top is pulled down and the bottom is pulled up. What is excessive is reduced; what is insufficient is supplemented. The way of heaven reduces excess and supplements deficiency. The way of people is different — they take from those who have too little and give to those who have too much. Who can offer the world their excess? Only the person of Tao.
