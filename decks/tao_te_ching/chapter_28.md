Know the masculine, but keep to the feminine — become a valley for the world. Being a valley, eternal virtue will not fade, and you return to the state of the infant. Know the white, but keep to the black — become the pattern of the world. Know glory, but keep to humility — become the valley of the world. Return to the uncarved block.
