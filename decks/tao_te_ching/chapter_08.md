The highest good is like water. Water benefits all things without competing with them. It settles in the low places that everyone else avoids. In this it is close to the Tao. Live in a good place, keep your mind deep, be generous, speak honestly, govern well, work skillfully, and act at the right time. Because you do not compete, no one can find fault with you.
