The Tao gives birth to one. One gives birth to two. Two gives birth to three. Three gives birth to the ten thousand things. All things carry yin and embrace yang, and through the blending of these forces find harmony. What others teach, I also teach: the violent do not die a natural death. This will be the essence of my teaching.
