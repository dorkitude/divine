Abandon holiness, discard cleverness, and the people will benefit a hundredfold. Abandon morality, discard righteousness, and the people will return to natural love. Abandon ingenuity, discard profit, and there will be no thieves. These three statements are not enough in themselves — they point toward something deeper: see the simple, embrace the natural, reduce selfishness, have few desires.
