The greatest virtue is to follow the Tao and the Tao alone. The Tao is shadowy, elusive, intangible — yet within it there is substance. Within it there is essence. That essence is very real; within it there is something that can be tested. From the beginning until now, its name has never been forgotten. How do I know the ways of all things at the beginning? Through this.
