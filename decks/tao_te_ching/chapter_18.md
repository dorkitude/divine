When the great Tao is forgotten, goodness and morality appear. When wisdom and cleverness emerge, great hypocrisy follows. When family relations are no longer harmonious, dutiful children are praised. When the country falls into chaos, loyal ministers appear. The virtues we celebrate are symptoms of what we have lost.
