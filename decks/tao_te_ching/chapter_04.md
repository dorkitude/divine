title: "The Tao Is an Empty Vessel"

The Tao is like an empty vessel — used but never filled. It is bottomlessly deep, the ancestor of all things. It blunts sharp edges, untangles knots, softens the glare, and settles the dust. It is hidden but always present. No one knows whose child it is — it appears to have preceded everything.
