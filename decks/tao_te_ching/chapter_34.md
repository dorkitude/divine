---
title: "The Great Tao Flows Everywhere"
number: 34
keywords: ["flowing", "nourishing", "humility", "greatness"]
asset_url: ""
---

The great Tao flows everywhere, both left and right. All things depend on it for life, and it does not turn away from them. It accomplishes its work but does not claim credit. It nourishes all things but does not lord over them. Because it does not claim greatness, it achieves greatness.
