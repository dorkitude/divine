Hold fast to the great image and all the world will come. It will come without harm, in safety, peace, and well-being. Music and good food make the passerby stop. But words about the Tao seem bland and flavorless. Look for it — it cannot be seen. Listen for it — it cannot be heard. Use it — it cannot be exhausted.
