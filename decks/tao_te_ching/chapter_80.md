---
title: "A Small Country with Few People"
number: 80
keywords: ["simplicity", "contentment", "small", "local"]

---

A small country with few people. Let there be tools tenfold or a hundredfold beyond what is needed, but let the people not use them. Let them regard death seriously and not travel far. Though there are boats and carriages, let no one ride them. Though there are weapons, let no one deploy them. Let the people return to knotting ropes instead of writing. Let them be content with their food, satisfied with their clothing, comfortable in their homes, happy with their customs. Neighboring countries can see each other, hear each other's roosters and dogs. Yet the people grow old and die without ever visiting one another.
