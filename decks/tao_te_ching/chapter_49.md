---
title: "The Sage Has No Fixed Mind"
number: 49
keywords: ["flexibility", "trust", "goodness", "openness"]
asset_url: ""
---

The sage has no fixed mind but takes on the mind of the people. To the good, I am good. To the not-good, I am also good, because virtue is goodness. To the trustworthy, I am trustworthy. To the untrustworthy, I am also trustworthy, because virtue is trust. The sage lives in the world gently, keeping their heart open.
