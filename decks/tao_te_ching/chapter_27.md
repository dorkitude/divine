title: "A Good Traveler"

A good traveler has no fixed plans and is not intent on arriving. A good speaker leaves no openings for attack. A good counter needs no tally. A good door needs no lock, yet cannot be opened. A good knot needs no rope, yet cannot be untied. The sage is always good at saving people and abandons no one, always good at saving things and abandons nothing. This is called following the light.
