---
title: "The World Cannot Be Improved"
number: 29
keywords: ["non-interference", "sacred vessel", "balance", "restraint"]
asset_url: ""
---

Those who wish to take the world and act on it will fail. The world is a sacred vessel that cannot be improved by force. Those who try to change it will ruin it. Those who try to hold it will lose it. Things sometimes lead and sometimes follow, sometimes blow hot and sometimes blow cold, sometimes are strong and sometimes weak. The sage avoids excess, extravagance, and extremes.
