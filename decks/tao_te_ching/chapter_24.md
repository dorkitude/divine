Those who stand on tiptoe do not stand firm. Those who rush ahead do not get far. Those who try to shine dim their own light. Those who define themselves cannot know who they really are. Those who cling to their work create nothing that endures. From the perspective of the Tao, these are like excess food and unnecessary baggage. Set them down.
