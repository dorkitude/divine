Nature says few words. A whirlwind does not last all morning. A rainstorm does not last all day. If heaven and earth cannot sustain things forever, how much less can human effort? Those who follow the Tao are one with the Tao. Those who follow virtue are one with virtue. Those who follow loss are one with loss.
