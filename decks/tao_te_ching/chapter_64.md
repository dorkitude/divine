What is at rest is easy to hold. What has not yet appeared is easy to prevent. What is brittle is easy to break. What is small is easy to scatter. Act before things exist. Create order before there is disorder. A tree that fills the arms grows from a tiny seedling. A tower nine stories high begins with a heap of earth. A journey of a thousand miles begins beneath your feet.
