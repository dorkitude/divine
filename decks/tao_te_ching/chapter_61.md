title: "A Great Country Is Like a Low-Lying Land"
number: 61
keywords: ["humility", "receptivity", "diplomacy", "lowliness"]
asset_url: ""
---

A great country is like low-lying land where rivers converge. It is the reservoir of the world, the feminine of the world. The feminine always overcomes the masculine through stillness and lowliness. Therefore a great country wins over a small country by placing itself below. A small country wins over a great country by placing itself below. Humility achieves what force cannot.
