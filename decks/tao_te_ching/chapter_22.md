Yield and you will overcome. Bend and you will stand straight. Empty yourself and you will be filled. Wear yourself out and you will be renewed. Have little and you will gain. Have much and you will be confused. The sage embraces the one and becomes a model for all. Because they do not compete, no one can compete with them.
