Great perfection seems imperfect, yet its use is inexhaustible. Great fullness seems empty, yet its use is endless. Great straightness seems crooked. Great skill seems clumsy. Great eloquence seems stammering. Stillness overcomes heat. Coolness overcomes agitation. Clarity and stillness set the whole world right.
