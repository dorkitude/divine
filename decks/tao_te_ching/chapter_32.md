The Tao is forever nameless. Though the uncarved block is small, no one can master it. If kings and lords could hold to it, all things would submit by themselves. Heaven and earth would unite and sweet dew would fall. People would live in harmony without being commanded. Once the block is carved, there are names. When there are names, know when to stop. Knowing when to stop averts trouble.
