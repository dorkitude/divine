The world has a beginning — consider it the mother of the world. Having found the mother, you know the children. Knowing the children, return to the mother. Close your mouth, shut your doors, and your life will be full. Open your mouth, increase your activities, and your life will be beyond hope. Seeing the small is insight. Holding to softness is strength. Use the light, but return to insight.
