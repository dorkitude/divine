title: "When People No Longer Fear Authority"
number: 72

When people no longer fear authority, a greater authority will arrive. Do not constrict their living space. Do not suppress their livelihood. If you do not oppress them, they will not tire of you. The sage knows themselves but does not show off. They love themselves but do not exalt themselves.
