The Tao gives birth to all things. Virtue nourishes them. Matter gives them form. Environment shapes them. Therefore all things respect the Tao and honor virtue. This respect and honor arise naturally, not by decree. The Tao gives birth, nourishes, develops, cares for, shelters, comforts, and protects — without possessing, without expecting, without controlling. This is called mysterious virtue.
