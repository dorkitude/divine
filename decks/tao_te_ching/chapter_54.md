---
title: "What Is Firmly Established Cannot Be Uprooted"
number: 54
keywords: ["cultivation", "virtue", "family", "community"]
asset_url: ""
---

What is firmly established cannot be uprooted. What is firmly held cannot slip away. Cultivate virtue in yourself and it becomes genuine. Cultivate it in your family and it overflows. Cultivate it in your community and it grows. Cultivate it in your country and it flourishes. Cultivate it in the world and it becomes universal.
