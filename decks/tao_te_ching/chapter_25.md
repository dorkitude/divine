There was something formless and complete that existed before heaven and earth. Silent and empty, it stands alone and does not change. It moves everywhere and is never exhausted. It may be regarded as the mother of all things. I do not know its name, so I call it Tao. Forced to give it a description, I call it great. Great means departing, departing means far-reaching, far-reaching means returning.
