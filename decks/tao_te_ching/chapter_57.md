Govern a country with straightforward methods. Wage war with surprise tactics. Win the world with non-interference. The more prohibitions you have, the poorer the people become. The more weapons you have, the more chaotic the state. The more clever the people, the stranger things become. The more laws you make, the more thieves there are. Therefore the sage says: I take no action and the people transform themselves.
