---
title: "The Virtue of an Infant"
number: 55
keywords: ["innocence", "vitality", "harmony", "newborn"]
asset_url: ""
---

One who is filled with virtue is like a newborn child. Wasps and snakes do not sting it. Wild beasts do not pounce on it. Its bones are soft, its muscles weak, but its grip is firm. It does not yet know the union of male and female, but its vitality is whole. It can cry all day without becoming hoarse — so complete is its harmony. Knowing harmony is knowing the eternal.
