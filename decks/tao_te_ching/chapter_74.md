title: "When People Do Not Fear Death"

When people do not fear death, how can you threaten them with death? If people lived in constant fear of death and we could seize and kill those who break the law, who would dare? There is always an executioner — nature itself. Trying to do the executioner's work is like trying to do the master carpenter's cutting. Rarely do you avoid injuring your own hands.
