---
title: "True Virtue Is Not Virtuous"
number: 38
keywords: ["virtue", "authenticity", "decline", "pretense"]
asset_url: ""
---

The highest virtue does not consider itself virtuous and therefore has virtue. The lowest virtue clings to virtue and therefore lacks it. The highest virtue acts without effort and without ulterior motive. When the Tao is lost, virtue appears. When virtue is lost, kindness appears. When kindness is lost, justice appears. When justice is lost, ritual appears. Ritual is the empty husk of faith.
