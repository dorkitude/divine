Rivers and seas can be kings of a hundred valleys because they lie below them. Therefore they can rule the hundred valleys. If the sage wishes to lead the people, they must speak humbly. If they wish to be first, they must place themselves last. The sage is above the people but they do not feel the weight. They are in front but the people are not harmed. The whole world supports them without tiring.
