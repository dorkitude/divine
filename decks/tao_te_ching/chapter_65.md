The ancient masters who practiced the Tao did not try to enlighten the people but to keep them simple. People are difficult to govern because they have too much knowledge. Those who use cleverness to govern a country are a curse. Those who do not use cleverness are a blessing. Understanding these two is understanding the pattern. Keeping the pattern always in mind is called mysterious virtue.
