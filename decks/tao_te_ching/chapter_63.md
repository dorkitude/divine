---
title: "Act Without Acting"
number: 63
keywords: ["non-action", "simplicity", "difficulty", "small beginnings"]
asset_url: ""
---

Act without acting. Work without effort. Taste the tasteless. Treat the small as great and the few as many. Respond to injury with virtue. Deal with the difficult while it is still easy. Accomplish the great task through a series of small acts. The sage never attempts great things and therefore achieves greatness.
