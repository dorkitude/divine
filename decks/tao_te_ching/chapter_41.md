---
title: "When the Wise Hear the Tao"
number: 41
keywords: ["understanding", "levels", "laughter", "paradox"]
asset_url: ""
---

When the wise hear the Tao, they practice it diligently. When the average hear it, they half-believe it. When the foolish hear it, they laugh out loud. If they did not laugh, it would not be the Tao. The bright path seems dim. The forward path seems to go backward. The smooth path seems rough. True virtue seems hollow. Great purity seems tarnished. The Tao is hidden and nameless, yet it alone nourishes and completes.
