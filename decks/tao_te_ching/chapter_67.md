Everyone says the Tao is great and seems different from everything else. It is great precisely because it seems different. I have three treasures that I guard and keep. The first is compassion. The second is frugality. The third is not daring to be first in the world. With compassion one can be courageous. With frugality one can be generous. Not daring to be first, one can lead.
