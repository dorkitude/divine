title: "True Words Are Not Beautiful"
number: 81
keywords: ["truth", "beauty", "giving", "conclusion"]

---

True words are not beautiful. Beautiful words are not true. Those who are good do not argue. Those who argue are not good. Those who know are not learned. Those who are learned do not know. The sage does not hoard. The more they do for others, the more they have. The more they give, the richer they become. The way of heaven is to benefit and not to harm. The way of the sage is to act and not to compete.
