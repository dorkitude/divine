My words are easy to understand and easy to practice. Yet no one in the world understands them or practices them. My words have an ancestry. My deeds have a master. Because people do not understand this, they do not understand me. Those who understand me are few, and therefore I am valued. The sage wears coarse clothing but carries jade within.
