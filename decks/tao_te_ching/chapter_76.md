---
title: "The Stiff and Unbending Is the Disciple of Death"
number: 76
keywords: ["flexibility", "hardness", "death", "life"]
asset_url: ""
---

When people are born, they are soft and supple. When they die, they are hard and stiff. When plants are alive, they are tender and flexible. When they die, they are brittle and dry. Therefore the hard and stiff are disciples of death. The soft and supple are disciples of life. An army that cannot yield will be defeated. A tree that cannot bend will break.
