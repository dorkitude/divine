The Tao is the treasure of the good and the refuge of the not-good. Beautiful words can be bought at the market. Noble deeds can earn respect. But even those who are not good — why should they be rejected? When a new leader is installed, do not send gifts of jade and fine horses. Instead, offer the Tao. Why did the ancients value the Tao? Because through it, seekers find and sinners are forgiven. That is why it is the greatest treasure.
