title: "Those Who Know Do Not Speak"

Those who know do not speak. Those who speak do not know. Close the mouth, shut the doors, blunt the sharpness, untangle the knots, soften the glare, settle the dust. This is called the mysterious union. One who has achieved this state cannot be approached through friendship or hostility, profit or harm, honor or disgrace. Therefore they are the most valued in the world.
