Abandon learning and there will be no sorrow. The difference between "yes" and "no" — how great is it really? Other people are bright and festive; the sage alone is dark and confused, drifting like the sea, blown like the wind. Everyone else has a purpose; the sage alone is stubborn and uncouth. But the sage alone values being nourished by the great mother.
