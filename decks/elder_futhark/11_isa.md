ᛁ — Isa
Phonetic value: I

Isa is ice — pure stillness, the frozen pause between actions. It represents a period where movement is neither possible nor advisable. Nothing grows on ice, but ice preserves. Use this time for inner concentration, for gathering yourself. The thaw will come, but forcing it serves nothing.
