ᚲ — Kenaz
Phonetic value: K

Kenaz is the torch that lights the darkness — the rune of knowledge, craft, and creative fire. It represents the controlled flame of skill and artistry, the light you carry into unknown places. This rune encourages learning, making, and bringing clarity to what has been obscured.
