ᚷ — Gebo
Phonetic value: G

Gebo is the rune of the gift — the sacred exchange between giver and receiver. In Norse culture, gift-giving created bonds of obligation and honor. This rune speaks to partnerships, reciprocity, and the balance required in all relationships. True generosity creates connection; it never diminishes the giver.
