ᛃ — Jera
Phonetic value: J/Y

Jera is the rune of the harvest, the completion of a year's cycle. Seeds planted long ago are finally bearing fruit. This rune reminds you that all worthwhile things take time — you cannot rush the seasons. The reward comes to those who have done the work and waited with patience for the turning of the wheel.
