ᛞ — Dagaz
Phonetic value: D

Dagaz is the dawn — the precise moment when darkness gives way to light. It represents breakthrough, radical clarity, and the kind of transformation that happens in an instant after long preparation. The night is over. What was confused is now clear. Act on this clarity before the momentum fades.
