ᚾ — Nauthiz
Phonetic value: N

Nauthiz is the rune of necessity and need — the friction that creates fire. Constraint is not punishment; it is the pressure that forges strength. This rune appears when resources are scarce and patience is required. Work with your limitations rather than against them, and let need itself become your teacher.
