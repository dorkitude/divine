ᛏ — Tiwaz
Phonetic value: T

Tiwaz is the rune of Tyr, the god who placed his hand in the wolf Fenrir's mouth knowing he would lose it — the ultimate act of honorable sacrifice. This rune represents justice, duty, and the courage to do what is right regardless of personal cost. Stand firm in your principles. The arrow points upward.
