ᛒ — Berkano
Phonetic value: B

Berkano is the birch tree — first to colonize cleared ground, symbol of new beginnings and gentle growth. It represents birth, nurturing, and the patient tending of things in their earliest stages. Something new is emerging in your life. Give it shelter, warmth, and time. Not everything needs to be forced into the light.
