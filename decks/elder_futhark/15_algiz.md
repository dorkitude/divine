ᛉ — Algiz
Phonetic value: Z

Algiz is the rune of protection — the elk's splayed antlers, the sedge grass that cuts anyone who grasps it carelessly. It represents a shield against harm, both physical and spiritual. When Algiz appears, you are being watched over, but you must also remain alert. Protection is not passivity; it is vigilant awareness.
