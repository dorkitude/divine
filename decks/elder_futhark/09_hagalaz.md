ᚺ — Hagalaz
Phonetic value: H

Hagalaz is the hailstorm — sudden, destructive, and beyond human control. It represents the disruptive forces that shatter what has grown brittle or complacent. There is no negotiating with hail. But after the storm, the ground is watered and the air is clean. Accept what cannot be changed and prepare to rebuild.
