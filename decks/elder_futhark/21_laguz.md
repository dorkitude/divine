ᛚ — Laguz
Phonetic value: L

Laguz is water in all its forms — the ocean's depth, the river's flow, the well of the unconscious mind. It represents intuition, dreams, and the currents beneath the surface of things. Logic alone will not serve you here. Let yourself be guided by what you feel but cannot yet articulate. Trust the deeper current.
