ᛊ — Sowilo
Phonetic value: S

Sowilo is the sun rune — radiant, life-giving, and impossible to extinguish. It represents victory, wholeness, and the guiding light that burns away confusion. The sun does not struggle to shine; it simply does. This rune calls you to step into your full power with the same natural confidence. Success is favored.
