ᛗ — Mannaz
Phonetic value: M

Mannaz is the rune of humanity — not the isolated individual, but the self understood in relation to others. It represents human intelligence, social bonds, and the shared experience of being alive. Know yourself, but know too that you are part of something larger. Your greatest insights will come through honest self-reflection.
