ᛟ — Othala
Phonetic value: O

Othala is the rune of ancestral property — the homeland, the inherited estate, the deep roots of belonging. It represents what has been passed down through generations: land, traditions, values, and identity. This rune asks you to honor where you come from while deciding what to carry forward and what to release.
