ᚱ — Raidho
Phonetic value: R

Raidho is the rune of the journey — both the physical road and the inner path of right action. It speaks to rhythm, order, and the natural progression of things. Movement is favored now, whether that means travel, a change of direction, or simply finding the correct tempo for the work at hand.
