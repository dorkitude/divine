ᛇ — Eihwaz
Phonetic value: EI

Eihwaz is the yew tree — ancient, poisonous, and evergreen. The yew bridges the worlds of living and dead, its roots reaching into the underworld while its branches touch the sky. This rune represents the axis of transformation, the passage through difficulty that leads to profound renewal. Endure. What feels like an ending is a doorway.
