ᚦ — Thurisaz
Phonetic value: TH

Thurisaz is the thorn — both weapon and defense. Associated with the giants and with Thor's hammer, it represents the reactive force that meets aggression with equal power. This rune signals a moment of friction or confrontation that, while uncomfortable, serves as a catalyst for necessary change.
