ᚹ — Wunjo
Phonetic value: W

Wunjo is the rune of joy and deep contentment — not fleeting pleasure, but the lasting satisfaction of things coming together as they should. It represents harmony within a clan or community, the warmth of belonging. When Wunjo appears, savor the goodness present in your life right now.
