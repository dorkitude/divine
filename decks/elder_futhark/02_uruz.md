ᚢ — Uruz
Phonetic value: U

Uruz channels the untamed strength of the aurochs, the wild ox of ancient Europe. It represents raw vitality, physical health, and the primal force that drives growth. When this rune appears, it calls you to tap into your deepest reserves of strength and to face challenges with the full weight of your being.
