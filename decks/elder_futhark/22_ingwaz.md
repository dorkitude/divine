ᛜ — Ingwaz
Phonetic value: NG

Ingwaz is the rune of the seed — potential contained, waiting for the right moment to emerge. Named for the god Ing (Freyr), it represents fertility, internal growth, and the quiet period of gestation before something is ready to be born. The work is happening beneath the surface. Do not disturb it. When it is ready, it will emerge on its own.
