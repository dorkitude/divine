ᛖ — Ehwaz
Phonetic value: E

Ehwaz is the horse — loyal companion, vehicle of swift travel, and symbol of the bond between rider and mount. This rune represents partnership built on mutual trust, steady forward progress, and the kind of movement that comes from working in harmony with another. You do not have to make this journey alone.
