ᚠ — Fehu
Phonetic value: F

Fehu is the rune of mobile wealth — cattle, gold, energy in motion. It speaks to abundance that must circulate to retain its power. Hoarded wealth stagnates; shared wealth multiplies. This rune asks you to consider what you possess and how freely it flows through your life.
