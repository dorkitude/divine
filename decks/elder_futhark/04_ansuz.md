ᚨ — Ansuz
Phonetic value: A

Ansuz is the rune of Odin, the Allfather — god of poetry, wisdom, and the spoken word. It represents divine communication, the breath of inspiration, and messages arriving from unexpected sources. Pay attention to signals, conversations, and insights that seem to come from beyond your ordinary thinking.
