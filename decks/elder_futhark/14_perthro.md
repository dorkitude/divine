ᛈ — Perthro
Phonetic value: P

Perthro is the dice cup, the vessel of fate and chance. Its exact meaning has been debated for centuries, which is fitting — it is the rune of mystery itself. Something hidden is at work in your situation. Outcomes are not yet determined. This is both uncertainty and possibility, the fertile unknown from which all futures emerge.
