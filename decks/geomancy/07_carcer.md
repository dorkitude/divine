Element: Earth | Planet: Saturn | Zodiac: Capricorn

Carcer is walls on all sides — restriction, confinement, and forced stability. This is not necessarily punishment; sometimes containment is protection. But movement is blocked, and attempts to break free will meet resistance. Accept the limitation for now. Use the confinement for focused inner work.
