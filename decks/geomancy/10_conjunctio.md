Element: Air | Planet: Mercury | Zodiac: Virgo

Conjunctio is the meeting point — the crossroads where paths converge. This figure represents unions, partnerships, contracts, and the combining of separate things into something new. Two forces are coming together. The outcome depends on what is being joined and whether the combination serves both parties.
