Laetitia — Joy

  •
  • •
  • •
  • •

Element: Air | Planet: Jupiter | Zodiac: Pisces

Laetitia points upward like a tower or a mountain peak — everything is rising. This is the figure of genuine joy, optimism, and success that feels earned. Laughter, celebration, and upward movement are all favored. The energy here is expansive and generous. Share your good fortune; it multiplies when given away.
