Puer — The Boy

  • •

Element: Fire | Planet: Mars | Zodiac: Aries

Puer is the young warrior — bold, energetic, and impatient. This figure brings raw courage and initiative but also recklessness and a tendency to act before thinking. The energy here is powerful but undisciplined. Channel it carefully, or it will create as many problems as it solves.
