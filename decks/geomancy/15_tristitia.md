Tristitia — Sorrow

  • •
  • •
  • •
  •

Element: Earth | Planet: Saturn | Zodiac: Aquarius

Tristitia points downward like a stake driven into the earth — things are sinking, declining, or being pulled toward their foundations. This is a figure of sorrow, but sorrow is not without purpose. Depth is only reached by going down. What you find at the bottom may be exactly what you need, however painful the descent.
