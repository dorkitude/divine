Element: Earth | Planet: Sun | Zodiac: Aquarius

Fortuna Major is the most favorable figure in geomancy — deep, stable success built on solid foundations. Unlike its lesser counterpart, this fortune endures. Protection, inner strength, and lasting achievement are all indicated. The victory here is not luck but the natural result of preparation meeting opportunity.
