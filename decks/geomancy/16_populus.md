Element: Water | Planet: Moon | Zodiac: Cancer

Populus is the crowd, the assembly, the collective. Double dots in every position make this the most neutral figure — it takes on the character of whatever surrounds it. This figure represents community, public opinion, and situations where individual will matters less than collective momentum. Read the room. The group has its own intelligence.
