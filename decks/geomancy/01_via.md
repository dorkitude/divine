Via — The Way

Element: Water | Planet: Moon | Zodiac: Cancer

Via is pure movement — a single column of dots like footprints on a road. Nothing stays fixed. Everything is in transition. This figure favors travel, change, and forward motion, but warns against trying to hold ground. The only constant here is change itself. Go with it.
