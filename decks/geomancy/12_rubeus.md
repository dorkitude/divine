Rubeus — The Red One

  •
  • •
  •
  • •

Element: Water | Planet: Mars | Zodiac: Scorpio

Rubeus burns red with passion, anger, or intoxication — powerful emotions that overwhelm reason. This figure warns of danger through excess, clouded judgment, and situations driven by desire rather than wisdom. Caution is required. The intensity here can be creative or destructive depending entirely on how it is channeled.
