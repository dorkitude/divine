Albus — The White One

  • •
  • •
  •
  •

Element: Water | Planet: Mercury | Zodiac: Gemini

Albus is clear and luminous — the white light of wisdom and peaceful contemplation. This figure favors study, counsel, spiritual practice, and any pursuit requiring clear thinking. It is not a figure of action but of understanding. Before you act, be sure you see clearly. The answer is available to the quiet mind.
