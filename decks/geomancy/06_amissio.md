Amissio — Loss

  • •
  •
  • •
  •

Element: Fire | Planet: Venus | Zodiac: Taurus

Amissio is the figure of loss — what you hold slips away. But loss is not always misfortune. Sometimes what departs needed to go. This figure warns against grasping tightly and suggests that releasing freely may be wiser than clinging. What is truly yours cannot be lost; what was never yours cannot be kept.
