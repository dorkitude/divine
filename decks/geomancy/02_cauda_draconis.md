Element: Fire | Planet: South Node (Saturn) | Zodiac: Sagittarius

The dragon's tail sweeps behind it, clearing everything in its wake. Cauda Draconis marks endings, departures, and the final chapter of a situation. What has been building is now concluding. This is not a time to begin new ventures but to finish old ones and release what no longer serves.
