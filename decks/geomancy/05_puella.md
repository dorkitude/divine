Element: Water | Planet: Venus | Zodiac: Libra

Puella represents beauty, receptivity, and harmonious relations. This figure favors diplomacy, art, pleasure, and gentle persuasion over force. It counsels patience and grace — let things come to you rather than grasping for them. Attractiveness of purpose draws allies more effectively than aggression.
