Fortuna Minor — Lesser Fortune

  • •
  • •
  • •
  •

Element: Fire | Planet: Sun | Zodiac: Leo

Fortune favors you, but briefly. Fortuna Minor is success that depends on speed and external circumstances rather than deep foundations. Take advantage of the moment while it lasts, but do not build permanent plans on temporary luck. Move quickly and decisively — the window is open but closing.
