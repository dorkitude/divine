An old man told Baizhang he had been reborn as a fox for five hundred lifetimes because he once told a student that an enlightened person is not subject to cause and effect. Baizhang said: "An enlightened person does not ignore cause and effect." The old man was released.

Not falling into cause and effect, not ignoring cause and effect — what is the difference?
