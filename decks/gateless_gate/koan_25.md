Yangshan dreamed he went to Maitreya's realm and was given the third seat. A senior monk struck the table and announced: "Today the one in the third seat will preach." Yangshan struck the table and said: "The truth of the Great Vehicle is beyond the four propositions and transcends the hundred negations. Listen!"

If you need permission to speak the truth, you have already lost it.
