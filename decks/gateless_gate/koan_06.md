Before a great assembly, the Buddha held up a single flower and said nothing. The entire gathering was silent and confused — except Mahakashyapa, who smiled. The Buddha said: "I have the treasury of the true dharma eye. I entrust it to Mahakashyapa."

What was transmitted without words?
