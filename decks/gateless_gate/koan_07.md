A monk told Zhaozhou: "I have just entered the monastery. Please teach me." Zhaozhou asked: "Have you eaten your rice porridge?" The monk said: "I have eaten." Zhaozhou said: "Then go wash your bowl."

The teaching is not hidden. It is right in front of you, wearing the clothes of ordinary life.
