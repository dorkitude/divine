---
title: "Bodhidharma Pacifies the Mind"
number: 41
keywords: ["mind", "searching", "peace", "unfindable"]
asset_url: ""
---

Huike said to Bodhidharma: "My mind is not at peace. Please pacify my mind." Bodhidharma said: "Bring me your mind and I will pacify it." After a long silence, Huike said: "I have searched for my mind, but I cannot find it." Bodhidharma said: "There — I have pacified it."

The disturbance cannot be found when you look directly at it.
