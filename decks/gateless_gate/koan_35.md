Goso asked: "Seijo's soul separated from her body. Which was the real Seijo?"

In the old Chinese tale, a woman's spirit split — one part stayed home sick, the other eloped with her lover. Years later they reunited and became one. Which was real? If you have ever felt divided against yourself, this is your koan.
