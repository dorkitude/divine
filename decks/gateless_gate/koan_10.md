Qingshui said to Caoshan: "I am solitary and poor. Please help me." Caoshan called out: "Qingshui!" Qingshui responded: "Yes?" Caoshan said: "You have already drunk three cups of the finest wine and you still say you have not moistened your lips."

You are never as empty as you think.
