Zhaozhou asked Nanchuan: "What is the Way?" Nanchuan said: "Ordinary mind is the Way." Zhaozhou asked: "Should I direct myself toward it?" Nanchuan said: "If you try to direct yourself toward it, you will move away from it."

The moment you aim at ordinary, it becomes extraordinary effort.
