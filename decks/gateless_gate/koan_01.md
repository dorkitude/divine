A monk asked Zhaozhou: "Does a dog have Buddha-nature?" Zhaozhou said: "Mu." (No/Nothing.)

Mumon's comment: To realize Zen, you must pass through the barrier of the patriarchs. Cut off the way of thinking. This one word "Mu" is the gateless gate itself. If you pass through it, you walk hand in hand with all the masters.
