Ananda asked Kashyapa: "The World-Honored One gave you the golden robe. Did he give you anything else?" Kashyapa called: "Ananda!" Ananda answered: "Yes?" Kashyapa said: "Knock down the flagpole at the gate."

The thing you are looking for is in the fact that you already responded.
