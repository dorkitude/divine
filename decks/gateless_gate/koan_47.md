Touzi set up three barriers: You beat the grasses and probe for the snake for the purpose of knowing the snake. Now I ask — what is the snake? Studying Zen means understanding your own nature. Right now — where is your nature? If you know your nature, you are free from life and death. When your eyes close for the last time, how will you be free?

Three questions. One answer. But you must live that answer, not think it.
