Deshan came to Longtan seeking instruction and stayed talking until late at night. Longtan said: "It is getting late, you should go." Deshan opened the door, saw it was dark, and said: "It is dark outside." Longtan lit a paper candle and handed it to him. As Deshan reached for it, Longtan blew it out. Deshan was enlightened.

What did he see in the dark that he could not see by candlelight?
