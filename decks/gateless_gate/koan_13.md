One day Deshan came to the dining hall carrying his bowls. Xuefeng said: "Old teacher, the bell has not rung and the drum has not sounded. Where are you going with your bowls?" Deshan turned and went back to his room. Xuefeng told Yantou, who said: "Old Deshan has not yet understood the last word of Zen."

Even the greatest masters still carry their bowls to meals that haven't been served.
