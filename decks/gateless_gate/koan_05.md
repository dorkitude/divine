Xiangyan said: "A person hangs from a tree branch by their teeth. Their hands cannot grasp a bough, their feet cannot touch the trunk. Someone below asks: What is the meaning of Bodhidharma coming from the West? If they do not answer, they fail the questioner. If they open their mouth, they fall and die." What do you do?

The real question is not about the tree.
