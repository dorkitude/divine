Guishan asked his monks to say something about a water jug without calling it a water jug. The head monk said: "You can't call it a wooden sandal." Guishan's cook kicked the jug over and walked out. Guishan gave the cook the leadership of the new monastery.

Sometimes the best answer is a well-placed kick.
