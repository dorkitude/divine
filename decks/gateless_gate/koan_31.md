Monks kept asking an old woman for directions to Mount Wutai. She always said: "Go straight ahead." When they walked forward, she would say: "Another fine monk going just like that." Zhaozhou went to investigate. He asked the same question, received the same answer, and returned saying: "I have seen through that old woman."

What did Zhaozhou see that the other monks missed?
