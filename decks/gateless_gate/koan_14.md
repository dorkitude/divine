The monks of the eastern and western halls were arguing about a cat. Nanchuan held up the cat and said: "If any of you can say a true word, you will save the cat. If not, I will cut it in two." No one could answer, and Nanchuan cut the cat.

Later, Zhaozhou returned and heard the story. He placed his sandals on his head and walked out. Nanchuan said: "If you had been there, the cat would have been saved."
