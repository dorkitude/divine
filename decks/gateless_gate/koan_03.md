Master Gutei answered every question about Zen by raising one finger. A boy who worked for him began imitating this gesture. Gutei seized the boy and cut off his finger. As the boy ran away crying, Gutei called after him. The boy turned, and Gutei raised his finger. The boy was enlightened.

What you borrow from others is never truly yours.
