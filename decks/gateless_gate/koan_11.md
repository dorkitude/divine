Zhaozhou visited two hermits. To each he said: "Are you there? Are you there?" The first raised a fist. Zhaozhou said: "The water is too shallow here." The second also raised a fist. Zhaozhou said: "Well given, well taken, well killed, well saved."

The same gesture, two different realities. What made the difference?
