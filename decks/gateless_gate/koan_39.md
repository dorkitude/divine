A monk said to Yunmen: "The radiance serenely illuminates the whole universe..." Before the monk could finish, Yunmen interrupted: "Aren't those the words of someone else?" The monk said: "Yes." Yunmen said: "You have missed it."

Borrowed words, however beautiful, are still borrowed.
