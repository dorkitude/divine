A fly bit a bald man on the head. He slapped at it and missed, striking himself instead. The fly laughed: "You were willing to kill yourself to avenge a fly's bite. What will you do to those who really injure you?"

**Moral:** An overreaction to a small annoyance causes more harm than the annoyance itself. Respond in proportion.
