A donkey saw how the master's lap dog was petted and fed treats for wagging its tail and sitting in laps. The donkey tried the same — putting his hooves on the master's shoulders and braying in his face. He was beaten and sent back to the stable.

**Moral:** What is charming in one creature may be terrible in another. Find your own strengths rather than copying someone else's.
