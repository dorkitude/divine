Two roosters fought for supremacy. The defeated one hid in a corner. The victor flew to the rooftop and crowed triumphantly. An eagle spotted the boasting rooster and carried him off. The quiet one emerged and took the flock.

**Moral:** Pride invites destruction. Announce your victories too loudly and you attract predators.
