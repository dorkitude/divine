A tortoise begged an eagle to teach him to fly. The eagle warned it was against his nature. The tortoise insisted. The eagle carried him high into the sky and released him. The tortoise fell to his death on the rocks below.

**Moral:** Not every ambition is appropriate. Some limitations exist for your protection. Know your nature.
