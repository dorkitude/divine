A once-mighty lion lay old and helpless. Animals he had once terrorized came to take revenge — the boar gored him, the bull charged him. Finally, a donkey kicked him in the face. The lion groaned: "I can endure the brave, but to be kicked by you — that is dying twice."

**Moral:** The cruelest blows come from those who attack only when you can no longer fight back.
