A horse and a donkey traveled together. The donkey, staggering under a heavy load, begged the horse to carry some of it. The horse refused. The donkey collapsed and died. The master then loaded everything — including the donkey's skin — onto the horse.

**Moral:** Refusing to share another's burden may result in bearing it all yourself.
