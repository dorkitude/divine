A thirsty crow found a pitcher with water at the bottom, too low to reach with its beak. One by one, the crow dropped pebbles into the pitcher until the water rose high enough to drink.

**Moral:** Necessity is the mother of invention. Patient ingenuity solves problems that brute force cannot.
