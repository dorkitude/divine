A wolf found a lamb drinking downstream and accused it of muddying his water. The lamb pointed out it was downstream. The wolf tried other pretexts, each easily refuted. Finally the wolf ate the lamb anyway, saying: "If not you, then your father wronged me."

**Moral:** A tyrant will always find a pretext for their tyranny. Reason means nothing to those who have already decided.
