title: "The Woodcutter and the Trees"

A woodcutter asked the trees for a branch to make a handle for his axe. The trees obliged. Once the axe was fitted, the woodcutter began felling the tallest trees. An old oak said to his neighbor: "We are destroyed by our own generosity."

**Moral:** Be careful what tools you give to those who may use them against you.
