A hare mocked a tortoise for being slow. The tortoise challenged the hare to a race. The hare, supremely confident, napped partway through. The tortoise plodded on without stopping and crossed the finish line first.

**Moral:** Slow and steady wins the race. Overconfidence is a greater handicap than any natural disadvantage.
