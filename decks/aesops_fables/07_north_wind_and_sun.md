The North Wind and the Sun argued over who was stronger. They agreed to test their power on a traveler, seeing who could make him remove his cloak. The Wind blew fiercely, but the man clutched his cloak tighter. The Sun shone warmly, and the man took off his cloak on his own.

**Moral:** Gentle persuasion achieves what force cannot. Warmth opens; pressure closes.
