A peacock spread his magnificent tail and mocked the crane's dull feathers. The crane replied: "You may trail your fine feathers in the dust, but I can soar above the clouds."

**Moral:** Usefulness outweighs beauty. Fine appearances are worthless if they serve no real purpose.
