A wolf had a bone stuck in his throat and offered a reward to anyone who could remove it. A crane reached her long neck down the wolf's throat and pulled out the bone. When she asked for her reward, the wolf said: "Your reward is that you put your head in a wolf's mouth and lived to tell about it."

**Moral:** Do not expect gratitude from those whose nature is ungrateful. Some debts will never be honored.
