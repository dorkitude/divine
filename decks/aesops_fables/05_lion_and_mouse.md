A lion caught a mouse and was about to crush it. The mouse begged for mercy, promising to repay the kindness someday. The lion laughed but let it go. Later, the lion was caught in a hunter's net. The mouse gnawed through the ropes and set the lion free.

**Moral:** No act of kindness is ever wasted. Even the smallest creature can repay a great debt.
