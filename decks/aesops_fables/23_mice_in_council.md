The mice held a council about the cat problem. A young mouse proposed hanging a bell around the cat's neck so they could hear it coming. All applauded — until an old mouse asked: "And who will bell the cat?"

**Moral:** It is one thing to propose a plan and another to carry it out. Any idea that cannot be executed is worthless.
