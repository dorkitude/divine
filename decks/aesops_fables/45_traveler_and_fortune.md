A traveler, weary from walking, lay down beside a deep well and fell asleep. Fortune appeared and woke him, saying: "If you had fallen in, you would have blamed me for your bad luck rather than your own carelessness."

**Moral:** We blame fortune for what our own negligence produces. Take responsibility before assigning blame.
