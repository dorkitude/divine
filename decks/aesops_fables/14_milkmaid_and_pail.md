A milkmaid carried a pail of milk on her head, daydreaming about what she would buy with the profits — eggs, then chicks, then a fine dress for the fair. Lost in fantasy, she tossed her head and spilled the milk.

**Moral:** Do not count your chickens before they hatch. Daydreaming about future gains distracts from present responsibilities.
