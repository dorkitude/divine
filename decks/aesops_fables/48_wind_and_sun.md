When the birds and beasts went to war, the bat sided with whichever group was winning. When peace was declared, both sides rejected the bat. He was banished to darkness, flying only at night.

**Moral:** Those who try to belong to every side end up belonging to none. Loyalty requires commitment, especially when it is costly.
