All summer the ant worked hard storing food for winter. The grasshopper sang and played, mocking the ant's labor. When winter came, the ant was warm and fed. The grasshopper, starving, begged for help.

**Moral:** There is a time for work and a time for play. Those who do not prepare for hard times will suffer when they arrive.
