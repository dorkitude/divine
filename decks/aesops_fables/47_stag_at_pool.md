title: "The Stag at the Pool"

---

A stag, drinking at a pool, admired the reflection of his magnificent antlers but despised his thin, ungainly legs. When hunters appeared, his legs carried him swiftly away — until his antlers caught in the branches and the hunters caught him.

**Moral:** What we admire in ourselves may be our undoing. What we despise may be our salvation.
