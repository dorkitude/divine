A dog carrying a bone crossed a bridge and saw his own reflection in the water below. Thinking it was another dog with a bigger bone, he snapped at the reflection. His own bone fell into the river and was lost.

**Moral:** Grasping for more than you have may cost you what you already possess. Do not mistake illusions for opportunities.
