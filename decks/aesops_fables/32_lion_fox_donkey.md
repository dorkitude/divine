A lion, fox, and donkey went hunting together and divided the prey. The donkey divided it equally. The lion killed the donkey. The lion asked the fox to divide. The fox gave nearly everything to the lion, keeping a tiny portion. "Who taught you such wisdom?" asked the lion. "The donkey," said the fox.

**Moral:** Learn from the misfortunes of others. A wise person does not need to suffer personally to gain understanding.
