A dying farmer told his sons there was treasure buried in their vineyard. After his death, they dug every inch of the land but found no treasure. That year, the vineyard produced the finest harvest anyone had ever seen.

**Moral:** The real treasure is the labor itself. Hard work is the greatest inheritance a parent can leave.
