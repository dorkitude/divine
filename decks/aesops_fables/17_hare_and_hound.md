A hound chased a hare but gave up after a long pursuit. A shepherd mocked the hound for being outrun by a small hare. The hound replied: "You forget — I was only running for my dinner. The hare was running for his life."

**Moral:** The one with more at stake will always work harder. Motivation makes the difference between effort and desperation.
