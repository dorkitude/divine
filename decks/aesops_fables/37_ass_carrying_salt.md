A donkey carrying salt fell into a river and discovered the salt dissolved, lightening his load. The next trip, he fell in deliberately. His master, wise to the trick, loaded him with sponges instead. The donkey fell in again — and staggered out under a load ten times heavier.

**Moral:** Tricks that work once may backfire spectacularly when repeated. Cleverness has limits.
