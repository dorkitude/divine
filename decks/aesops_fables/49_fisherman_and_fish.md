A fisherman caught a small fish. "Please throw me back," said the fish. "I will grow bigger, and then you can catch me again for a better meal." The fisherman replied: "A small fish in hand is worth more than a large fish in the sea."

**Moral:** A certain small gain is worth more than an uncertain large one. Do not trade the real for the merely possible.
