A cat hung himself from a peg by his hind legs, pretending to be dead. The mice crept out to celebrate. An old mouse said: "I have seen enough bags and traps in my day. I would not trust that bag even if it were stuffed with straw."

**Moral:** Experience teaches caution. Those who have survived traps before are not easily fooled by new ones.
