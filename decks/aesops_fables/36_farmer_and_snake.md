A farmer found a snake nearly frozen to death and placed it inside his coat to warm it. When the snake revived, it bit the farmer. As he lay dying, the farmer said: "I should have known that a snake is still a snake."

**Moral:** Kindness does not change the nature of the recipient. Some creatures will harm you regardless of your generosity.
