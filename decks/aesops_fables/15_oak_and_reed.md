title: "The Oak and the Reed"

---

A great oak mocked the thin reeds for bending in every breeze. A mighty storm came. The oak stood rigid and was torn up by its roots. The reeds bent flat, then sprang back when the storm passed.

**Moral:** Flexibility survives what rigidity cannot. The ability to bend is not weakness — it is wisdom.
