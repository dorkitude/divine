A country mouse visited her city cousin and marveled at the fine food. But every meal was interrupted by dangers — cats, servants, traps. The country mouse returned home, preferring her simple food in peace.

**Moral:** Better a modest life lived in safety than a luxurious life lived in fear. Peace of mind outweighs abundance.
