A fox invited a stork to dinner and served soup on a flat plate. The stork could not eat with her long beak. Later, the stork invited the fox and served food in a tall, narrow jar. The fox could not reach the food.

**Moral:** Treat others as you wish to be treated. Those who play tricks should expect tricks in return.
