The hands, mouth, and teeth grew angry at the belly for sitting idle while they did all the work. They refused to feed it. But soon they grew weak themselves, for without the belly processing food, no part of the body could function.

**Moral:** Every part of a system contributes, even those whose work is not visible. Refuse cooperation and everyone suffers.
