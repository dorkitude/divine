A fox lost his tail in a trap. Ashamed, he called a meeting and argued that all foxes should cut off their tails — they were useless and burdensome. An old fox replied: "You would not give us this advice if you still had yours."

**Moral:** Distrust advice that happens to benefit the advisor. Self-interest often hides behind general recommendations.
