A shepherd boy amused himself by crying "Wolf!" and watching the villagers run to his aid. After several false alarms, a real wolf appeared. The boy screamed for help, but no one came. The wolf devoured the flock.

**Moral:** Liars are not believed even when they tell the truth. Trust, once broken, is not easily rebuilt.
