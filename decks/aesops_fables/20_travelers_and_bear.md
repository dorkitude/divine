Two friends were traveling when a bear appeared. One quickly climbed a tree. The other, unable to flee, lay on the ground and played dead. The bear sniffed him and left. The man in the tree climbed down and asked what the bear had whispered. "He said never travel with a friend who abandons you in danger."

**Moral:** A true friend is proven in times of crisis, not comfort.
