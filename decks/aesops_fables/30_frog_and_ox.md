A frog saw an ox and, envious of its size, puffed himself up. "Am I as big as the ox?" he asked his friends. "Not yet," they said. He puffed more. And more. Until he burst.

**Moral:** Trying to be something you are not will destroy you. Accept your own scale and work within it.
