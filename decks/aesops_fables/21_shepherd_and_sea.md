A shepherd sold his flock and became a merchant, shipping goods across the sea. A storm destroyed his cargo. He rebuilt his flock, sold it again, and again lost everything at sea. When the sea was calm and beautiful, the shepherd said: "I know what you want — you want more sheep. You will not get them."

**Moral:** Some lessons must be learned twice. The lure of greater profit can destroy what you already have.
