title: "The Crow and the Swan"
number: 35

asset_url: ""
---

A crow envied the swan's white feathers and believed they came from washing in lake water. The crow moved to the lake and washed himself constantly, but his feathers stayed black. He starved, having abandoned his old feeding grounds for nothing.

**Moral:** Changing your environment does not change your nature. Chasing someone else's gifts may cost you your own.
