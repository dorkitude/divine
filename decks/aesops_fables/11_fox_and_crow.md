A crow sat in a tree holding a piece of cheese in her beak. A fox, wanting the cheese, praised the crow's beauty and begged to hear her lovely voice. The vain crow opened her beak to sing, and the cheese fell to the fox.

**Moral:** Beware of flattery. Those who praise you excessively usually want something from you.
