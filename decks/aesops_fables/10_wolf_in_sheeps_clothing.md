A wolf draped himself in a sheepskin and slipped into the flock. He feasted nightly on the unsuspecting sheep until the shepherd, planning to slaughter a sheep for dinner, unknowingly chose the disguised wolf.

**Moral:** Appearances deceive. Those who disguise their true nature eventually find that the disguise betrays them.
